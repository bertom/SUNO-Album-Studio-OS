# Style Learnings — Global

---

## Positive prompting in Styles

Tell SUNO what to do. Put hard avoids in Exclude styles.

---

## One rhythmic center

Every prompt should imply one main groove.

---

## Groove as feel, not costume

One rhythmic center per song — movement that serves the lyric, not genre cosplay on every track.

---

## Life beats concept

Musically alive imperfect version beats conceptually correct dead version.

---

## Album clothes vs artist body

Albums may shift genre, production era, or mood — preserve the artist's DNA markers from `01_artist/artist_identity.md` (vocal identity, rhythmic feel, emotional truth, etc.). What stays is defined by the artist, not this repo.

---

## Style blocks are starting points

See `01_artist/style_reference_summary.md` — adapt per song brief, never copy blindly.
