# Cover Prompt — Gentle Landscape Family

Calm, open, hopeful — natural world as mood, not a fixed recipe.

**Use when:** `visual_direction.md` calls for softness, space, dawn/dusk, or quiet scale (not every album — pick from the album story).

Guide: `01_artist/cover_design_guide.md`

---

## Before you generate

Read `album_art/visual_direction.md` and `cover_notes.md`. Fill the fields below from **this album** — do not copy a preset scene.

| Field | Your album (fill in) |
|-------|----------------------|
| Visual anchor | _[e.g. horizon line, single tree, window light, distant hills]_ |
| Key elements | _[2–4 concrete objects from your story]_ |
| Palette | _[from visual direction — not a default gold/amber]_ |
| Mood | _[one line — e.g. "quiet hope after rain"]_ |

---

## Composition defaults

| Field | Starting point |
|-------|----------------|
| Medium | Natural photography or soft illustration — match `visual_direction.md` |
| Layout | Lower third clear for title in post; image breathes above |
| Texture | Soft depth, gentle grain or bokeh — avoid HDR gloss |
| Typography | Handwritten or minimal sans — add in post unless testing type-in-image |

---

## Story seeds *(examples — replace with album-specific lines)*

- Open sky — room to arrive, not to explain.
- Small figure, large landscape — intimacy inside scale.
- After rain — clean air, path forward.

---

## Image prompt skeleton

Adapt every bracket from your album docs:

```text
[Your scene from visual_direction.md], [time of day and light],
[key elements from table above], [palette], natural photography,
shallow depth of field, calm atmosphere, no text, no watermark, photorealistic
```

## Negative prompt

```text
text, letters, watermark, logo, religious symbols, neon, cyberpunk, cartoon,
stock wellness cliché, oversaturated, harsh flash, album mockup frame, busy collage
```
