# Cover Prompt — Nature / Trust Family

Oneness. Reeds in wind. Flock of birds. Light on water. Trust we are part of something bigger.

Used for: gentle, child-safe, hopeful, trust-themed albums.

Guide: `01_artist/cover_design_guide.md`

---

## Family defaults

| Field | Default |
|-------|---------|
| Light / anchor | Low natural sun on horizon over water — golden hour photo |
| Composition | Photo with lower-third text zone; image breathes above |
| Key elements | Reeds foreground, birds mid-sky, water horizon |
| Texture | Soft bokeh, gentle lens flare, shallow depth of field |
| Palette | Amber, gold, cream, pale blue sky edge |
| Typography | Handwritten lowercase title; small-caps artist below |
| Mood | Safe, dreamy, recognition without explanation |

---

## Story prompts (pick or adapt)

- We belong to something larger. The flock knows.
- Reeds bend. Light holds. It will be alright.
- Trust — not as slogan, as feeling.

---

## Image prompt template

```text
[Scene from story prompt], golden hour natural photography, reeds in foreground,
soft murmuration of birds in distance, calm water horizon, warm amber and cream tones,
shallow depth of field, gentle bokeh, no text, no watermark, photorealistic
```

## Negative prompt

```text
text, letters, watermark, logo, religious symbols, neon, cyberpunk, cartoon,
stock photo smile, oversaturated, harsh flash, album mockup frame
```
