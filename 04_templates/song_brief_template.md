# Song Brief

Use this template for `/song brief`. One song, one emotional center, one postcard.

---

## Title

Working title. Final title may change after SUNO or listening.

---

## Life Source

Where did this song come from in real life? Memory, conversation, body feeling, season, image. Not abstract theme only.

---

## Album Role

What job does this track do on the album? (opener, mirror, mantra, release, landing, fire, etc.)

Reference `track_map.md` role column.

---

## Emotional Center

One dominant feeling or movement. If you need "and also," narrow down.

---

## One-Sentence Truth

The sentence the song proves or reveals — not an essay thesis.

---

## Listener Should Feel

After the last note — specific, embodied outcome.

---

## Core Image

One visual or physical image that anchors the song.

---

## Hook / Mantra

Repeatable phrase or rhythmic hook. Draft language welcome.

---

## Energy

Low / mid / high — plus texture words (e.g. "calm authority," "embodied joy," "sharp restraint").

---

## Rhythm / Groove

One rhythmic center. Examples: straight 4/4 pocket, half-time swing, four-on-the-floor pulse, syncopated funk, sparse half-time breathe.

---

## Vocal Direction

Lead gender preference, delivery mode, group vocal needs. Link to `01_artist/vocal_identity.md` if helpful.

---

## artist DNA To Preserve

Which 2–3 DNA markers must survive genre clothes? (e.g. dry vocal, bass movement, stacked harmonies, distorted guitar texture — from `artist_identity.md`)

---

## What This Song Is

Plain language. 2–4 bullets or short paragraph.

---

## What This Song Is Not

Critical for scope control. What are we refusing?

---

## Album Contrast

How does this differ from the track before and after? Energy, palette, vocal, or role.

---

## Risks

What could go wrong? (too conceptual, too mellow, SUNO default, preachy, etc.)

---

## Next Action

Single clearest next step: lyrics / style directions / SUNO v1 / listen to reference / etc.

---

## Agent Notes (optional)

Leave blank unless agent adds session context. Not copied to SUNO.
